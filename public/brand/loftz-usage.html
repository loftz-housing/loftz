LOFTZ — logo usage

Traced 1:1 from the commissioned artwork · clear space, minimum size, colour.

Clear space

X = the radius of the O-ring. Keep at least X of empty space on every side of the logo (and the mark). Nothing — type, edges, other logos — should intrude into this zone.

Minimum size

Wordmark — min 24 px tall (≈ 77 px wide) on screen
Mark — min 16 px (favicon)

Below these sizes the smile and ring counter start to fill in. For anything smaller than 16 px, use the mark, never the wordmark.
